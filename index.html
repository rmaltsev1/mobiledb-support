Getting started

  • Try the demo — tap Try Demo on the first screen to explore a built-in sample database. No account, no server, nothing to set up.
  • Add a connection — host, port, database, user, password. Credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • TLS — require, allow self-signed, or disable, per connection.
  • SSH tunnel — reach databases behind a bastion; the app opens the tunnel itself, with trust-on-first-use host-key pinning.
  • Browse — schema → table → row, with search, sort, filtering, and foreign-key jumps.
  • Query — SQL editor with autocomplete, history, saved queries, and CSV/JSON export.
  • Dashboards — build quick charts on your phone: a total count, a trend over time, or a breakdown by category — no SQL required (or write your own). Charts render natively.
  • Stay safe — tag a connection as prod (red banner) and open it read-only when you just want to look.

FAQ

Can I try it without a database? Yes — tap Try Demo for a built-in, in-memory sample database. Browse the sample tables and explore a ready-made dashboard with no connection needed.

Where do my credentials go? Into the iOS Keychain on your device. There is no backend — the app connects straight from your phone to your database.

Does it send my data anywhere? No. See our Privacy Policy.

Which databases are supported? PostgreSQL today. The data layer is engine-agnostic, so more may come.

Can't connect? Check that your server is reachable from the device's network, TLS mode matches your server, and—if using SSH—that the tunnel host and key are correct.
