Privacy Policy

MobileDB · Last updated 16 June 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how MobileDB ("the app") handles your information. In short: the app does not collect, store, or share any personal data, and there is no backend server.

No data collection

The app does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, usage analytics, or advertising identifiers. There are no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Your database connections

Connection details (host, port, database, username, and options) are stored locally on your device. Passwords and SSH private keys are stored in the iOS Keychain. None of this is ever transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Direct connections only

MobileDB connects directly from your device to the database servers and SSH hosts that you configure. Your queries, results, and any data you view or edit travel only between your device and your own servers. The developer operates no servers and never sees this traffic. Connections use TLS and/or an SSH tunnel as you configure them.

Data stored on your device

Saved connections, query history, and saved queries are stored locally on your device only. Deleting the app removes this data.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
